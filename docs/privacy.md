**Memora** is a complete platform for preserving and delivering digital memories.  
Privacy protection and user control over data are core principles of the product.

We are not a social network. We do not trade data.  
Memories remain personal.

---

### 1. Who we are
Memora provides digital infrastructure for:
- storing memories (photos, videos, audio, text)
- organizing them into Capsules
- future or selective access by third parties

The user remains the **sole owner of the content**.

---

### 2. Data we process

#### 2.1 Account data
- Email address
- Account settings and preferences

#### 2.2 User content
- Photos, videos, audio files
- Texts, notes, descriptions
- Metadata defined by the user

User content:
- is not used for advertising
- is not analyzed for profiling
- is not sold or shared with third parties

#### 2.3 Technical & operational data
- Security logs
- IP address in limited or anonymized form
- Data necessary for service operation

---

### 3. Processing principles
Processing is based on:
- **Lawfulness & transparency**
- **Data minimization**
- **Purpose limitation**
- **Privacy by design & by default**

---

### 4. Privacy & access
- All content is **private by default**
- No public exposure occurs without explicit user action
- Third-party access is granted only:
  - by invitation
  - via roles
  - with time-based or functional restrictions

---

### 5. Time-based & inheritance access
Users may define:
- when content is revealed
- to whom
- under which conditions

Memora acts as a **technological memory custodian**, not as a content owner or interpreter.

---

### 6. Data security
Technical and organizational measures include:
- Encryption of data in transit
- Restricted infrastructure access
- Per-user data separation

No digital service can guarantee absolute security, but data protection remains a continuous priority.

---

### 7. Data subject rights (GDPR)
Users have the right to:
- access
- rectification
- erasure
- data portability
- restriction of processing

Requests may be submitted via the contact details.

---

### 8. Data retention
- Data is retained while the account is active
- Upon account deletion, content is deleted or anonymized within a reasonable timeframe

---

### 9. Third-party providers
Infrastructure providers (hosting, storage) are used solely for technical operation.  
They are not permitted to use data for independent purposes.

---

### 10. Policy updates
This policy may be updated.  
Significant changes will be communicated within the platform.
